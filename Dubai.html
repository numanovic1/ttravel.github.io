Welcome to Dubai

Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant nightlife. Here are some of the must-visit attractions in Dubai:

Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, standing at a height of 828 meters. Visitors can enjoy stunning views of the city from the observation deck on the 148th floor.

Palm Jumeirah

The Palm Jumeirah is an artificial island shaped like a palm tree. It is home to luxury hotels, resorts, and private residences. Visitors can relax on the pristine beaches or indulge in various water activities.

Dubai Mall

Dubai Mall is one of the largest shopping malls in the world, featuring numerous high-end stores, entertainment attractions, and a wide range of dining options. It is also adjacent to the Burj Khalifa.

Old Dubai

Explore the historic side of Dubai by visiting the Al Fahidi Historic District and the Dubai Museum. Take a traditional abra (boat) ride across Dubai Creek and wander through the bustling souks (markets).
